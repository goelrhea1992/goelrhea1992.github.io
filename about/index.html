Hi, there. I would best describe myself as a thinker; curious, and a seeker by nature.

I do not float with the tide, but I do not swim for A goal either. Unlike most people of the 21st century, I am not ambitious. I'm rather passionate, about several little things.

Each one of us is constantly changing, in our own different way. Our persepectives change, and goals reshape. Why would I chase something so temporary?

I went to IIIT Delhi, for my undergraduate studies in Computer Science and haven't looked back ever since. I am currently pursuing my Master's degree at Columbia University, New York.

I've always strived for the most bizarre things in life, with the gut feeling that "I might just get it". Am I the luckiest person on Earth or what?
